Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SMR Motors collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect
  • Personal Information: Name, mobile number, and login details.
  • Bike Listing Information: Model, price, kms, location, condition, and uploaded images.
  • Usage Data: IP address, device info, browser type, pages visited.
  • Cookies: To remember login sessions and preferences.
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your bike listings publicly.
  • To contact you for enquiries or updates.
  • To improve website performance and user experience.
  • To protect the website from fraud or misuse.
3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Passwords are securely stored and never shared. However, no system is 100% secure.

4. Sharing of Information
  • We do not sell or rent your information.
  • We may share information with trusted service providers (hosting, database, email services).
  • Listings and contact details may be shown to the public for enquiry purposes.
  • We may share information if required by law or legal process.
5. User Rights
  • You can request to update or delete your account.
  • You can request a copy of the personal data we store.
  • You can ask us to stop using your information for certain purposes.
6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and localStorage to remember your login and to keep your browsing smooth. You can clear them anytime in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page.
